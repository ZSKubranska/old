Blob Thrower 2

Game Description:
Blob Thrower is back for more fun puzzle action. Your goal is to bring the 2 yellow blobs together. To do this, you must aim the thrower and launch small black blobs to push the larger blobs into place or interact with any objects or obstacles on the level. Play through 50 increasingly challenging levels.

Game Instructions:
Use your mouse to aim and left-click to fire a blob. Right-click on the thrower to remove any black blobs on the screen.
